[[za128rs]]
=== Za128rs Extension for Reservation-Set Size, Version 1.0

[[norm:za128rs_res_set_req]]
The Za128rs extension requires that the reservation sets used by the
instructions in the Zalrsc extension be contiguous, naturally aligned, and at
most 128 bytes in size.

[[za64rs]]
=== Za64rs Extension for Reservation-Set Size, Version 1.0

[[norm:za64rs_res_set_req]]
The Za64rs extension requires that the reservation sets used by the
instructions in the Zalrsc extension be contiguous, naturally aligned, and at
most 64 bytes in size.

[[norm:za64rs_implies_za128rs]]
The Za64rs extension implies the Za128rs extension.
