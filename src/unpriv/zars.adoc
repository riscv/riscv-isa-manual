[[ext:za128rs]]
=== ext:za128rs[] Extension for Reservation-Set Size

[[norm:za128rs_res_set_req]]
The ext:za128rs[] extension requires that the reservation sets used by the
instructions in the ext:zalrsc[] extension be contiguous, naturally aligned, and at
most 128 bytes in size.

[[ext:za64rs]]
=== ext:za64rs[] Extension for Reservation-Set Size

[[norm:za64rs_res_set_req]]
The ext:za64rs[] extension requires that the reservation sets used by the
instructions in the ext:zalrsc[] extension be contiguous, naturally aligned, and at
most 64 bytes in size.

[[norm:za64rs_implies_za128rs]]
The ext:za64rs[] extension implies the ext:za128rs[] extension.
