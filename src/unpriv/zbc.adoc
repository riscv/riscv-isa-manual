[[ext:zbc]]
=== ext:zbc[] Extension for Carry-less Multiplication

Carry-less multiplication is the multiplication in the polynomial ring over GF(2).

[#norm:zbc_clmul_clmulh_results]#insn:clmul[] produces the lower half of the carry-less product and insn:clmulh[] produces the upper half of the 2{times}XLEN carry-less product.#

[#norm:zbc_clmulr_results]#insn:clmulr[] produces bits 2{times}XLEN−2:XLEN-1 of the 2{times}XLEN carry-less product.#
